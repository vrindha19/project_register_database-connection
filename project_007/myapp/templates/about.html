veg & fruit

They are naturally good and contain vitamins and minerals that can help to keep you healthy. They can also help protect against some diseases.

Fruits come from the flowering part of a plant and contain seeds. In contrast, vegetables are the edible parts of a plant, such as the leaves, stem, roots, and bulbs.

Fruit and vegetables should be an important part of your daily diet.

A diet high in fruit and vegetables can help protect you against cancer, diabetes and heart disease.

ruit and vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium
